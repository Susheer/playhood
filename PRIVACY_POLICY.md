# Privacy Policy for PlayHood

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-28

## 1. Introduction

PlayHood is a free music practice and playback app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the app.

## 2. Who Can Use the App

PlayHood is available to anyone. There are no restrictions for downloading or using the app.

## 3. What Users Can Do

Users can:

- Load audio files from their device.
- Play, pause, and seek audio.
- Loop specific segments of a song.
- Adjust playback speed (tempo).
- Use the mini player and full player screen.

## 4. Ads and Payments

- PlayHood is completely free.
- The app does not display ads.
- No in-app purchases or subscription fees are required.

## 5. Data Collection

- PlayHood does not collect personal information.
- No usage tracking or analytics are included.
- All audio files are loaded locally from your device.

## 6. Audience

- Ideal for singers, music learners, or anyone who wants to practice or study music efficiently.
