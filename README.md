**PlayHood** is a free music practice and playback app designed for singers, music learners, and anyone who wants to study or enjoy music in a controlled way. It allows users to loop specific segments of a song, adjust playback tempo, and play music seamlessly on Android devices.

## Features

- **Play local audio files**: Load songs from your device storage.
- **Loop segments of a song**: Select a portion of any audio file and play it repeatedly.
- **Adjust playback speed**: Slow down or speed up audio without changing pitch.
- **Mini player on Home Screen**: Keep the current song playing while browsing your library.
- **Full player screen**: For advanced controls including looping and tempo adjustments.
- **Background playback**: Audio continues even when the app is minimized or device is locked.
- **Free and ad-free**: No in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads.

## How to Use

1. **Load Songs**: Tap "Load Audio Files" or pick a file from your device.
2. **Play/Pause**: Tap the play icon next to a song to start or pause playback.
3. **Mini Player**: Use the mini player at the bottom to control playback without leaving the Home Screen.
4. **Loop & Tempo**: Navigate to the Player Screen for loop segment and tempo controls.
5. **Background Playback**: You can navigate away or minimize the app, and the music keeps playing.

## Privacy Policy

- **Who can use this app**: PlayHood is free and available for anyone.
- **What you can do**: Users can load songs, play, loop, and adjust tempo of audio files stored on their device.
- **No ads or payments**: PlayHood does not display ads, track users, or require payment.
- **No data collection**: The app does not collect personal data or share any information.
- **Target audience**: This app is ideal for singers, music learners, and anyone who wants to practice or study music efficiently.
